MaleficHat Bot

MaleficHat is designed to track players' performances in League of Legends. It allows users to register a player using their Riot ID and retrieve information about their recent ranked games in Solo/Duo. The bot automatically monitors the matches of registered players and sends a summary of the statistics at the end of each game, including K/D/A, damage dealt, and game duration.

Users can also view the last 10 ranked games of a player, organized in a clear format with information about their rank (tier, rank) and league points (LP). Additionally, users can create a personalized leaderboard with their friends to see who is the best!

Players have the option to disable all functionalities at any time.
